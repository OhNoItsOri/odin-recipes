Chicago Style Stuffed Crust Pizza

Description

What do you love about pizza? Its Pizza! A big circle with meat. It's got the JUICE. OH and the list of ingredients was too long. Since I am lazy I will be doing this for frozen pizza.

But if you are interested here is the recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Oven
  • 1 Frozen pizza
  • 1 Will to live this will be consumed in the cooking process

Steps

  1. Put heat up to at least 375 fahrenheit -- up to a maximum of 450 fahrenheit
  2. Place pizza onto baking sheet, and place that whole thing in the oven
  3. Wait for 20 - 25 minutes
  4. Trade your soul to a demon in exchange for the pizza
  5. Enjoy the pizza
